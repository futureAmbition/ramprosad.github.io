About Me

My name is Ram Prosad Sarker

Ram Prosad Sarker is a dedicated undergraduate student in the Department of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU), Tangail, Dhaka, Bangladesh, with a strong passion for machine learning, deep learning, neural networks, cybersecurity, and mobile development. His research focuses on developing predictive models with techniques and Explainable AI, exploring advanced neural network architectures, and enhancing information system security. He actively participates in competitive programming to refine his problem-solving skills and is also engaged in Android development projects, with a keen interest in iOS development. Proficient in Python, Java, C, and C++. Ram Prosad Sarker utilizes tools such as TensorFlow, Keras, and Android Studio to work on innovative projects and contribute to the fields of machine learning and cybersecurity

B.Sc. (Engineering) in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Institution: Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University (MBSTU), Tangail, Dhaka, Bangladesh

My hobby is traveling.
